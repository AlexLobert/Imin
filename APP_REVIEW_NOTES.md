## Steps To Review
1. Log in with the demo account above.
2. Go to `Connect`:
   - Toggle In/Out.
   - Tap `Visible to` to choose `Everyone` or a circle.
3. Go to `Chats`:
   - Start a new chat from `In Now` or the `+` create sheet.
   - Send a message.
4. Safety:
   - In a chat, tap `…` → `Report`.
   - In a chat, tap `…` → `Block`.
5. Account deletion:
   - Go to `Profile` → `Delete account` and confirm.

## Notes
- This app includes user-generated content (chat). Users can **Report** chats/messages and **Block** users in the chat screen.
- Reports are reviewed in the Supabase dashboard (table: `public.content_reports`).
